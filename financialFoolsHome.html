The future value calculator is used to calculate the future value of an investment based off of it's present value, time-horizon, anticipated interest rate, and the number of compounding periods.


The budget calculator is used to compare an individual's income to their expenses, hopefully allowing them to better understand their personal balance sheet and increase savings over time.


The financial ratio calculators are used to calculate corporate financial ratios, stemming from corporate financial statements, to better understand the health and financial stability of a corporation.


The mortgage calculator is used to calculate the monthly mortgage payment amount based off of the cost of the home, down payment, annual percentage rate, property taxes, and term length of the loan.
